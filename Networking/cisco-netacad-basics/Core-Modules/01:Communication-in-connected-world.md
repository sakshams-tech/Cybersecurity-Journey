# __NETWORK TYPES:__

## What are Networks?
Networks are system of interconnected computing devices (nodes) such as computers, servers and routers that exchange data with each other.

Networks can be of 2 types.
1. Public Network(Internet): Network of Networks, open to everybody.
2. Private / Local Network: Restricted network, access only to authorised users.

## Internet:
Internet is a collection of Interconnected networks that nobody owns. Everything that we access, like social media, multiplayer games and online courses, we do it using the internet. Internet can be
accessed using an ISP (Internet Service Provider).

## Local Networks:
Local Networks can be of many sizes. They can range from only 2 devices to hundreds or even thousands of devices. Most of these networks provide shared connection to the internet.
They can be classified as follows:

1. Small Home Networks: These networks connect a few devices to eachother and to the internet. These can be insecure these days.
2. Small Office / Home Office(SOHO) Networks: It allows the devices to connect to the Corporate local network remotely. 
3. Medium to Large Networks: These are often seen in School and Corporations where thousands of devices are connected to each other via the same network.

## Mobile Devices:
Internet can also connect devices other than laptops and workstations, such as Devices you use daily.

1. Smart Phone: Smart phones can connect to the internet almost anywhere either through cellular network or wifi. 
2. Tablets: They are like smart phones, the only big difference is that they come with a bigger screen.
3. Smart Watch: These can be connected to you Smart phone and can track your heart rate, view your messages etc..

## Home Devices:
Many Devices in your home can also be connected to the internet. These include:
1. Security System 
2. Appliances
3. Smart TV
4. Gaming console
